Mind, Brain, and Education (MBE) science: How translational research is improving education

Tom Gorham (he, his, him)

Tom Gorham, Rissho University

Abstract

Mind, Brain, and Education (MBE) science is a transdisciplinary field that blends psychology, neuroscience, and education with the goal of improving the practice of teaching and learning. MBE was started at Harvard University but now it has practitioners around the world. In this unconference session I would like to share some of the work that my colleagues and I have been conducting over the past decade to bring MBE science to educators through academic conferences, workshops, and publications. Furthermore, I would like to explore the possibility of connecting the Neuromatch Conference/Academy community with our MBE community.
