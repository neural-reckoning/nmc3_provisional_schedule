Social: Movie Watch Party of "Back to the Future"

Abstract

Neuromatch Movie Watch Party! Come have fun and relax with everyone over the time-travel classic, ‘Back to the Future.’ We will use the service Scener as a virtual movie theater to watch the film together using your Netflix account. Note that you must have an individual Netflix account to participate. Go to https://scener.com/ and create a free account before showtime.

We’ll be interacting in the chat while the movie airs, so join in with colleagues and friends from the past and make some future ones!
