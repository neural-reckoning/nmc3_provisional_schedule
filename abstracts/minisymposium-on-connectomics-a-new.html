Minisymposium on Connectomics: a new era of dissemination and discovery

Abstract

Over the past fifteen years, the field of connectomics has developed in three overlapping stages. The first two have focused on the technical challenges of data collection and automated reconstruction. Now the third and most exciting stage is picking up speed. This symposium will describe connectomic resources that are being disseminated now, and the discoveries that they are enabling.
