Black In Neuro Mentorship Workshops: Getting the most out of your mentor-mentee relationship (Trainee workshop)

Abstract

Note: zoom links for this event will not be posted until shortly before the session. Please refresh the website right before the meeting for the current link.

In this interactive workshop, we will examine the concept of “mentoring up”. Through discussion and case study analysis we will outline effective strategies trainees can use to strategically find and maintain strong mentoring relationships and build communication skills as a means to enhance self-advocacy in your research training and beyond.
