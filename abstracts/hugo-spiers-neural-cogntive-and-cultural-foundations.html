Neural, cogntive and cultural foundations of navigaiton ability

Hugo Spiers

Abstract

Some people are very good at navigating, others find it a significant challenge. What determines whether someone will succeed or fail to navigate successfully? This talk will briefly explore what might make someone a good navigator looking at demographics, cultural background and brain structure and function.
