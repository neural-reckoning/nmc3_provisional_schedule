Black In Neuro: Neuroracism Panel Discussion

Abstract

Black In Neuro will lead a panel discussion as the first installment of a 2-part event on neuroracism. The NeuroRacism panel will center discussions on how we in academia collect, use and interpret data about Black people. Panelists will highlight ongoing biases and anti-Black racism that is currently embedded in the practices of neuro-related research. We will explore what it means to think about data critically, from a social justice oriented lens, and to question the current status quo.
