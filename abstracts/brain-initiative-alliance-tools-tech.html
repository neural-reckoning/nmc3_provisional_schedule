BRAIN Initiative Alliance Tools, Tech, Theory, and Trainee Series: Theory (part 3)

Abstract

The BRAIN Initiative Alliance (http://www.braininitiative.org/) is hosting a virtual series in collaboration with Neuromatch 3.0 to highlight cutting-edge tools, technology, and theories emerging from the US BRAIN Initiative. The event is divided into three session to focus on 1) Tools (probes, optics, cellular, molecular), 2) Tech (software, hardware), and 3) Theory (modeling, computation), with trainees integrated in each session. Each session will include five brief talks/demos showcasing new tools, techniques, or theories from US BRAIN Initiative toolmakers followed by a moderated discussion with Q/A.

This is session 3/3, focused on Theory (modeling, computation).
