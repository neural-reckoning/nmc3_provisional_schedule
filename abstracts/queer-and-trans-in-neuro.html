Queer and Trans in Neuro: Story session and Q&A panel

Abstract

Queer and Trans in Neuro will lead a session of sharing stories and a panel Q&A about being LGBTQ in neuroscience. Moderator: John Krakauer (Johns Hopkins University) Panelists: - Joni Wallis (University of California, Berkeley) - Bruno Olhausen (University of California, Berkeley) - Aniruddha Das (Columbia University) - Daniel Virga (Columbia University) - Kaela Singleton (Emory University)
